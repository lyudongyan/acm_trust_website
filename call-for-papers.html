Paper Submission and Publication

Manuscripts must be submitted in PDF format using the two-column ACM sigconf template. Full papers may be up to 9 pages, including appendices, with unlimited pages permitted for references and the GenAI Usage Disclosure section. Reviewing will be double-blind, so submissions must not include author names or affiliations and should avoid obvious self-references. Accepted papers will be published in the ACM Digital Library and indexed in EI Compendex and Scopus.
