Overview

Trustworthy systems, from foundations to deployment

ACM TRUST 2027 brings together researchers, practitioners, policymakers, and system builders working on trustworthy and responsible AI and computing systems. The conference addresses the technical and societal requirements for systems that are secure, resilient, transparent, accountable, and aligned with human values.

REED-AI focuses on advancing trustworthy AI methodologies and deployment frameworks for healthcare and clinical environments. As AI increasingly influences clinical decision-making, medical imaging, healthcare operations, and patient outcomes, systems must be transparent, robust, accountable, safe, and aligned with ethical and regulatory requirements.
